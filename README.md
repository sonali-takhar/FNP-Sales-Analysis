# FNP-Sales-Analysis

This Excel-based Sales Dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of sales performance, revenue trends, customer purchasing behavior, and product category contributions. The dashboard transforms raw sales data into actionable insights through interactive visualizations, Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, and Slicers.

The primary objective of this project is to help business stakeholders monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), identify revenue-driving occasions and product categories, and make data-driven decisions to improve sales performance.

## Dashboard Highlights

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* **Total Orders:** 1,000
* **Total Revenue:** ₹35,20,984
* **Average Revenue per Order:** ₹3,520.98
* **Average Delivery Time:** 5.53 Days

### Business Insights

#### Revenue by Occasion

The dashboard analyzes revenue generated across different occasions such as:

* Anniversary
* Raksha Bandhan
* Holi
* Birthday
* Diwali
* Valentine's Day

Among these, Anniversary and Raksha Bandhan contribute the highest revenue, indicating strong seasonal demand.

#### Monthly Revenue Trends

Monthly sales analysis reveals revenue fluctuations throughout the year, with noticeable peaks during festive and gifting seasons. This helps businesses identify high-performing periods and plan marketing campaigns effectively.

#### Revenue by Product Category

The dashboard compares revenue across multiple product categories, including:

* Colors
* Sweets
* Soft Toys
* Cakes
* Plants
* Mugs

The analysis highlights top-performing categories and identifies areas for product portfolio optimization.

#### Revenue by Order Time

Hourly revenue tracking provides insights into customer purchasing behavior and peak ordering periods, enabling better operational and promotional planning.

#### Geographic Analysis

The dashboard identifies the top cities by order volume, helping businesses understand regional demand patterns and optimize distribution strategies.
